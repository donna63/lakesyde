Messaging Terms & Conditions

Secure Text Communication

Lakesyde Behavioral Health uses secure, HIPAA-compliant texting services to communicate with patients as needed. Our messaging system is designed to provide convenient communication while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

Important Notes

  • Message frequency may vary based on your treatment needs
  • Message and data rates may apply (check with your carrier)
  • For help at any time, reply HELP
  • To opt out at any time, reply STOP

Contact Guidelines

For administrative questions or scheduling, feel free to text our administrative line:

702-602-7626

Important: Please avoid sending any clinical or sensitive information via text. For clinical matters, please use our secure patient portal or call our office directly.

Information Collected

To provide you with the best possible care and communication, we may collect the following information:

Personal Information

Name and contact details for identification and communication

Contact Information

Phone number, electronic and physical address for coordination

Service Information

Other relevant contact details and service preferences

Communication Records

Records of communication for continuity of care

Use of Information Collected

The information we collect is used exclusively to enhance your care experience and facilitate effective communication:

Service Delivery

To deliver requested services, including appointment reminders, scheduling, and treatment coordination

Administrative Functions

For billing, payment processing, and other administrative operations

Customer Service

To provide responsive customer service and address your inquiries

Care Coordination

To coordinate with other healthcare providers as needed for your comprehensive care

Sharing of Information Collected

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We take your privacy seriously and only share information when absolutely necessary for your care or as required by law.

Authorized Service Providers

Information may be shared only with authorized service providers (e.g., secure messaging platforms, payment processing systems) as necessary to perform operational duties.

Healthcare Coordination

With your consent, information may be shared with other healthcare providers involved in your treatment for coordinated care.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your information to third parties.

We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not use your information for any purpose other than your care and administrative needs.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.

HIPAA Compliance & Security

HIPAA-Compliant Communication

Your privacy is protected by healthcare privacy laws

Text messages are transmitted through HIPAA-compliant platforms designed specifically for healthcare communication.

Important Security Notice: For sensitive or clinical matters, we strongly recommend using our secure patient portal or phone communication. While our text platform is secure, it's best to reserve texts for administrative purposes only.

Text messages are primarily limited to administrative purposes unless explicit consent is provided for clinical communication via text.

Best Practices for Secure Communication:
  • Use text only for appointment reminders, scheduling, and non-sensitive administrative matters
  • Use our secure patient portal for clinical questions, medication discussions, and treatment updates
  • Call our office directly for urgent matters or complex clinical discussions

Opt-Out Options & Support

Control Your Communication Preferences

To Stop Receiving Texts

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from text messages. You will receive a confirmation text, and no further messages will be sent.

For Assistance

Reply HELP for instructions on how to use our texting service or to get support with your communication preferences.

Additional Support Options

Phone Support

Call our office directly during business hours for immediate assistance with any questions or concerns about your communication preferences.

Email Support

Send us an email through our secure patient portal for non-urgent questions about privacy or communication.

Note: Opting out of text messages will not affect your ability to receive care from Lakesyde Behavioral Health. You can still communicate with us via phone, secure patient portal, or in-person visits.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices or would like more information about how we protect your personal information, please don't hesitate to contact us.
