Insurance & Payment

Major Insurance Plans Accepted

We work with most major insurance providers through Headway platform, making quality mental healthcare accessible and affordable.

Appointments, Insurance & Private Pay

Lakesyde Behavioral Health offers both insurance-based care and private pay appointments, all scheduled through our secure patient portal.

If you prefer to self-pay or are not using insurance, you may schedule and pay directly with Lakesyde Behavioral Health.

We are currently in network with the following plans: Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare / Optum and Nevada Preferred Health Care Professionals. Appointments using these plans are scheduled directly through Lakesyde Behavioral Health and billed using your insurance benefits.

Clients who prefer to self-pay, or whose insurance is not listed above, may also schedule private pay appointments using the same scheduling link.

All appointments insurance and private pay are scheduled through our secure Tebra patient portal. Insurance eligibility and payment options will be reviewed after scheduling and prior to your appointment.
