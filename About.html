My Story & Vision

From Service to Healing

Leke Adedotun, MSN, APRN, PMHNP-BC is the Founder and Lead Practitioner of Lakesyde Behavioral Health. He has extensive experience providing compassionate, patient-centered mental health care to individuals from diverse backgrounds, including veterans, high-achieving professionals, young adults, parents, and those navigating trauma or major life transitions.

His journey into mental health care began during his service in the U.S. Army, where he witnessed firsthand the critical need for accessible, empathetic, and effective mental health support. That experience continues to shape his clinical approach one that integrates evidence-based psychiatric care with genuine human connection, respect, and understanding.

“I created Lakesyde as a place where patients feel seen, understood, and supported. Every session is calm, respectful, and centered on your story. My goal is to help you build clarity, balance, and emotional strength at a pace that feels right for you.”

Leke Adedotun, MSN, APRN, PMHNP-BC
Founder & Lead Practitioner, Lakesyde Behavioral Health

Following the completion of advanced nursing education and specialized training in psychiatric mental health, Mr. Adedotun has worked across a range of clinical settings, including inpatient psychiatric units and outpatient care. Throughout his career, he has remained dedicated to building strong therapeutic relationships rooted in trust, collaboration, and individualized treatment planning.

The vision of Lakesyde Behavioral Health is to provide a supportive, professional environment where healing feels attainable, care is personalized, and long-term emotional wellness is achievable.

Our Treatment Philosophy

Whole-Person Care

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. Mental health is interconnected with physical health, relationships, work, and overall life satisfaction. Our approach considers all aspects of your wellbeing.

  • Integrated mind-body approach to healing
  • Consideration of lifestyle, nutrition, and environment
  • Collaboration with other healthcare providers when needed

Evidence-Based Compassion

Our evidence-based approaches combined with genuine compassion have helped thousands of individuals and families find healing and recovery. We stay current with the latest research while maintaining the human touch.

  • Latest psychiatric research and best practices
  • Personalized treatment plans for each patient
  • Continuous professional development and training
