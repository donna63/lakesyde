About Lakesyde Behavioral Health

I'm Leke Adedotun, I'm a Board Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner, U.S. Army Veteran, Serving ages 6+ through telehealth and in person visits and founder of Lakesyde Behavioral Health . I’ve had the honor of working with diverse populations across Nevada, including veterans, high achieving professionals, young adults, parents, and individuals navigating trauma or major life transitions. I created Lakesyde as a place where patients feel seen, understood, and supported. Every session is calm, respectful, and centered on your story. My goal is to help you build clarity, balance, and emotional strength at a pace that feels right for you.

We believe in treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. Our evidence based approaches combined with genuine compassion have helped thousands of individuals and families find healing and recovery.
