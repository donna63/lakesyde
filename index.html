About Lakesyde Behavioral Health

Lakesyde Behavioral Health is a Nevada based telehealth mental health practice founded by Leke Adedotun, MSN, APRN, PMHNP-BC, a Board-Certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and U.S. Army Veteran. The practice provides compassionate, high-quality mental health care to individuals ages 6 and older through telehealth.

Mr. Adedotun has extensive experience working with diverse populations throughout Nevada, including veterans, high-achieving professionals, young adults, parents, and individuals navigating trauma or significant life transitions. Lakesyde Behavioral Health was created to be a safe, supportive space where every patient feels seen, heard, and respected.

Each session is approached with calm professionalism and genuine care, ensuring treatment is centered on the individual's unique experiences and goals. The mission of Lakesyde Behavioral Health is to help patients develop clarity, balance, and emotional resilience at a pace that feels comfortable and empowering.
